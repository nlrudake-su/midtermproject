About Me

During my time at Syracuse University, I have held numerous internships with both large and small organizations, gaining experience in strategic communications, social media, multimedia storytelling and more.

Outside of class, I write articles for Syracuse University’s Whitman School of Management, serve as a community ambassador for the university’s Office of Off-Campus and Commuter Services, and volunteer in the greater Syracuse area with Alpha Phi Omega, a national community service fraternity.

As a military brat, I moved frequently around the United States and Germany. In my spare time, I enjoy reading, running and cooking.
